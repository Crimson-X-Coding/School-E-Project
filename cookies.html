Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience.

We use essential cookies for website functionality, analytics cookies to understand user behavior, and advertising cookies to show relevant content.

Cookies help us remember your preferences, improve website performance, and deliver personalized content or ads.

Some cookies may come from third-party services such as Google Analytics. These are used for analytics and advertising purposes.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some website features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.
