Oxford Grammar School

Complete Information & Admission Details

About School

Oxford Grammar School is one of the leading educational institutions in Karachi. The school follows the Cambridge curriculum and focuses on academic excellence, discipline, and character building along with modern teaching techniques.

Facilities

  • Cambridge Curriculum
  • Modern Science & Computer Labs
  • Fully Equipped Library
  • Sports Ground & Indoor Activities
  • Secure Campus with CCTV
  • Transport Facility Available

Admission Process

Admissions open from December to February each year. Parents are required to submit the admission form along with necessary documents. An entrance test and interview may be conducted for selected grades.
