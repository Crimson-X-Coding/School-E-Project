About City Public School

City Public School is committed to providing high-quality education with modern teaching methodologies. The school promotes discipline, leadership, and character building along with strong academic performance.

Facilities

  • Air Conditioned Smart Classrooms
  • Computer & Science Laboratories
  • Well-Stocked Library
  • Indoor & Outdoor Sports Area
  • Modern Canteen Facility
  • Safe Transport Service

Admission Process

Admissions are open from December to February. Parents must submit required documents including previous school records. An entrance test may be conducted for middle and high school grades. Limited seats are available every year.
