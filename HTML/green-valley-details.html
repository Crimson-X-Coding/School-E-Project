Green Valley School

Complete Information & Admission Details

About School

Green Valley School provides high-quality education with modern classrooms, qualified teachers, and a focus on character building. The school encourages academic excellence along with extracurricular activities.

Facilities

  • Smart Classrooms
  • Science & Computer Labs
  • Library with Digital Resources
  • Sports Ground & Indoor Games
  • Modern Canteen
  • Transport Facility Available

Admission Process

Admissions are open from January to March every year. Parents must submit the admission form along with required documents. An entrance assessment test may be conducted for higher grades.
